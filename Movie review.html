Jaya-Jaya-Jaya-Jaya-Hey

Jaya Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey is a 2022 Indian Malayalam-language family comedy-drama film directed by Vipin Das.[3] The film stars Darshana Rajendran and Basil Joseph. Jaya Jaya Jaya Jaya Hey was released theatrically on 28 October 2022.[4] The sarcastic film about improper adulting received highly positive reviews from both critics and audiences
